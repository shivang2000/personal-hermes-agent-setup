**Software Engineering & DevTools**
- [Making 768 servers look like 1 — PlanetScale Postgres sharding walkthrough](https://planetscale.com/blog/making-768-servers-look-like-1) — End-to-end design doc: why read-replicas hit a ceiling (WAL bottleneck, fixed data capacity), the case for sharding, how a Vitess-style proxy makes routing transparent, and where cross-shard operations get ugly. Useful framing before you add "just spawn_blocking" to your runtime.
- [LLM networking with MikroTik](https://blog.greg.technology/2026/07/14/llm-networking-with-mikrotik.html) — Practical guide to running inference workloads on MikroTik-routed home/SMB networks (VLANs, queues, firewall rules for GPU rigs). Niche but unusually concrete for a homelab AI setup.
