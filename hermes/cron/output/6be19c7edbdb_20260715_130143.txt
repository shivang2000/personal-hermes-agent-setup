- **Tailscale SSH shipped a root-bypass in v1.98.9** — [TS-2026-009](https://tailscale.com/security-bulletins): Linux Tailscale SSH accepted usernames with a leading `-`, which `getent(1)` parsed as flags. Connecting as `-i` made Tailscale open an interactive `root` session in violation of `autogroup:nonroot` ACLs. Patch is 1.98.9 (also fixes TS-2026-007, a separate Tailscale Services packet-filter issue). Upgrade now if you use Tailscale SSH.
